Tor: FAQs

The Tor Technical FAQ Wiki is user-editable, meaning anyone can make edits to the content. We encourage Tor users and supporters to go fix it up, add more questions, provide answers, etc. While we will monitor the Wiki page to help ensure accuracy, the Tor developers are not responsible for the content.

The Tor Legal FAQ is written by EFF lawyers. It aims to give you an overview of some of the legal issues that arise from the Tor project.

The Abuse FAQ for Tor Server Operators is a collection of common questions and issues discussed when running a Tor server.
